## 🌐 What is REWARA?

**REWARA (RWA)** is a global reward and loyalty token built on **Base** (Ethereum L2), designed to connect communities through a transparent and decentralized reward system.

> *"Traditional loyalty programs give you points that expire. REWARA gives you tokens that are truly yours."*

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🏆 **On-Chain Rewards** — Earn RWA for contributions, referrals, and engagement
- 🌍 **Global & Borderless** — No geographic restrictions
- ⚡ **Powered by Base L2** — Ultra-low gas fees, fast transactions
- 🔒 **Transparent & Trustless** — All reward logic on-chain
- 🤝 **Community First** — 50% of supply for community rewards
- 🔄 **Utility-Driven** — Redeem RWA for platform perks and benefits
